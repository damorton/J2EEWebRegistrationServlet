Global Voices


Global Voices in Education showcases case studies, workshops and keynote addresses to amplify effective methods teachers, lecturers and practitioners have used to enhance the resonance and reach of their own best practice and to energise student voices in creative classrooms and online activities.


This day-long event celebrates the tenth anniversary of the ICT in Education conference series, an annual showcase of education leaders, opinion makers, and contributors to the #edchatie online discussions. Its highly regarded framework provides a place where enthusiastic educators can meet in person, sustaining relationships that span across three levels of education.
